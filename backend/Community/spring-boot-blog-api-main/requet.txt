Requêtes pour manipuler le backend et les requêtes appropriées dans Postman

1. Créer un utilisateur
Method: POST
URL: http://localhost:8080/users
Body:

{
  "email": "user@example.com",
  "userName": "username",
  "password": "password"
}



2. Créer un article
Method: POST
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles?userId=1
Body:
{
  "title": "Article Title",
  "slug": "article-title",
  "body": "This is the body of the article."
}


3. Obtenir tous les articles (page par défaut de 10)
Method: GET
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles

4. Filtrer les articles par tag
Method: GET
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles?tag=stocks

5. Filtrer les articles par auteur
Method: GET
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles?author=arnavg

6. Pagination des articles
Method: GET
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles?page=3&size=10

7. Obtenir un article par son slug
Method: GET
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles/article-title

8. Modifier un article par son slug
Method: PATCH
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles/article-title
Body:
{
  "title": "Updated Article Title",
  "body": "This is the updated body of the article."
}

9. Obtenir tous les commentaires d'un article
Method: GET
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles/article-title/comments

10. Ajouter un commentaire à un article
Method: POST
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles/article-title/comments?userId=1
Body:
{
  "title": "Comment Title",
  "body": "This is the body of the comment."
}

11. Supprimer un commentaire d'un article
Method: DELETE
URL: http://localhost:8080/articles/article-title/comments/1

12.afficher le contenue d'un article

Method: GET
http://localhost:8080/articles/treqt/content
